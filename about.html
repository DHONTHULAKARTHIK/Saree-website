Our Story

K. Lakshmanna Pure Silk Sarees was born from a deep love for the ancient craft of hand-loom weaving. Rooted in the silk-weaving capital of Dharmavaram, Andhra Pradesh, our journey began with a simple promise — to keep the tradition of pure silk alive for generations to come.

Every saree that leaves our workshop carries the soul of our weavers — skilled artisans who pour years of mastery into each thread, each motif, and each intricate border. From the luminous Kanchipuram to the vibrant Paithani, every piece tells a story of heritage and excellence.

We believe a saree is not just fabric — it is an emotion, a celebration, a legacy passed from mother to daughter. That is why we craft each one with the utmost care, using only the finest pure silk and genuine zari.

The Person Behind the Craft

Meet Our Founder

K. Lakshmanna

Founder & Master Weaver

With decades of experience in the art of hand-loom silk weaving, Sri K. Lakshmanna has dedicated his life to preserving the rich textile heritage of Andhra Pradesh. Operating from the heart of Dharmavaram, he established this venture to bring the finest handcrafted silk sarees directly to discerning customers across India. His vision — that every woman deserves to wear a saree woven with love, skill, and pure silk — drives everything we do.
